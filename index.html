Survival Basics

Move and fight Use WASD, aim with the mouse, reload with R, dash with Shift, and throw grenades with H or right click.

Open locked routes Kill the keycard carrier, unlock the security door with F, then pull the lever to activate the next-level pad.
Exploit traps Barrels explode when shot, wire slows zombies, generators arc electricity, and turrets fire on nearby enemies.
Spend between waves Use the shop after a cleared wave to buy weapons, modules, armor, ammo, grenades, and movement upgrades.
